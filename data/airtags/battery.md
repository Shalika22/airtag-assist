# Battery and replacement

AirTag uses a replaceable coin-cell battery. When the battery is low, Find My can show a low-battery indication. Replacing the battery generally involves twisting the stainless-steel back to open the AirTag, swapping the coin cell, then closing it again. If the AirTag doesnâ€™t reconnect after replacement, confirm the battery is seated correctly and the contacts are clean.

For long-term storage, remove the battery to prevent leakage.
